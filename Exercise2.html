The Wolf Among Us

A dark, gripping interactive story based on the *Fables* comics.

Overview

*The Wolf Among Us* is a choice-based narrative game developed by Telltale Games. Set in a gritty, noir-inspired world, players take on the role of Bigby Wolf, the sheriff of Fabletown, investigating a brutal murder that threatens the community.

Key Information

  • Developer: Telltale Games
  • Release Date: October 11, 2013
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Mobile
  • Genre: Interactive Drama, Adventure

Cast & Creative Team

  • Director: Nick Herman
  • Writer: Pierre Shorette
  • Bigby Wolf: Adam Harrington
  • Snow White: Erin Yvette

Characters

  • Bigby Wolf: The sheriff of Fabletown, trying to keep the peace.
  • Snow White: Assistant to the mayor, determined to help Fabletown.
  • Ichabod Crane: The mayor, but is he truly in control?
  • The Crooked Man: A criminal mastermind pulling the strings.
