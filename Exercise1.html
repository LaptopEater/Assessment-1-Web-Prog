Recipe for Baked Mac N Cheese

List of Ingredients Needed:

  • Macaroni
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Spices

Steps To Make Baked Mac n Cheese:

  1. First Grab your bowl
  2. Make the spaghetti sauce first by mixing in tomato sauce and all kinds of spices you wish
  3. Boil the Macaroni until edible
  4. Mix the spaghetti sauce and macaroni
  5. Melt Cheese sauce and pour onto the macaroni and spaghetti
  6. Place more unmelted cheese onto the top of the tray of baked mac n cheese
  7. Heat in the oven for 400 F* for 30 minutes
  8. Enjoy!!
